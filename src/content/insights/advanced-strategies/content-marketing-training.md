-   **Distribution Channels**: Utilizing platforms such as social media, blogs, and email to disseminate content.
-   **Audience Engagement**: Strategies to foster interaction and build community around the content.
-   **Analytics and Measurement**: Using tools to track performance and measure success.

An intimate understanding of the target audience is critical for creating content that resonates. Training in audience analysis involves learning how to:

-   Identify audience needs, pain points, and content consumption preferences.
