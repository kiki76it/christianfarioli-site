### Pizza and Google: many similarities

I choose pizza because I find a lot of similarities between pizza and Google. What? That's crazy. Pizza is made with **simple ingredients (**apparently). I'm sure that even in Africa, Vietnam, you will find those ingredients in ever grocery store. **It's very cheap to produce, some of the ingredients are free** (ex. water, salt) and it's **impossible to make it 100% perfect**. No matter how many times you go to the same pizzeria, you order 10 pizzas and **every pizza looks a little different**. Google is a set of very different tools apparently. One of the tools you use it on a daily basis, Google Search. When we go to Google there is a lot more and we're going to see a lot others. It's **very cheap to use**. Most of Google **tools are free**, so even cheaper than pizza. It's impossible to make it perfect. So, pizza and Google are very similar for these reasons:

-   very simple ingredients
-   very cheap to produce (almost free)
-   impossible to make it 100% perfect!
-   always changing

For example, about search engine optimization and analytics, **every few months something is changing**. The same thing working before, now doesn’t work anymore. Even if you do the same things, something has changed: exactly like pizza. This is why I tell you that digital marketing is a little bit more than just learning a skill. Digital marketing is a big subject, nowadays. **Once you find out what makes you passionate, you have to go into it for a long time, because it's something that's going to continuously change**. It doesn’t exist (1) that you learn everything and then in 10 years it's still going to be as it is. No way! Because of that it makes sense to dig in.  

### So, what is Digital Marketing?

Let’s see what Digital Marketing is. Are you ready? Here you will find three definition: the first comes from Google, the second from an important Institute but… the best is the last one!

> _1\. “Digital marketing is the process of building and maintaining customer relationships through online activities to facilitate the exchange of ideas, products, and services that satisfy the goals of both parties.”  (Google)_

How do I know that? I don’t now. I secretly Googled it and this is what came out. But as you are here on my blog, let's see a better definition.

> _2\. “Digital marketing is the use of digital channels to promote or market products and services to consumers and business.”  (Digital Marketing Institute)_

We'll say that. It was said by the Digital Marketing Institute, but I'm going to give you a better definition which is this:

> _3\. “Digital marketing is getting customers using only the computer and Internet.”  (Christian Farioli)_

Now you understand why is the best one, right? My definition is exactly like pizza: simple, free and always changing.
