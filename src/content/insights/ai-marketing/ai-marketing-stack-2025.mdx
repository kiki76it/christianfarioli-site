# The AI Marketing Stack for 2025

> **Executive Summary:** The marketing AI stack has consolidated. After two years of vendor chaos, the seven layers that matter are clear — and three categories you may be paying for are now overhyped. Here's the practitioner's map.

## The Seven Layers That Matter

1. **Discovery & AISO** — Tools that make your brand citable in AI answer engines.
2. **Content generation** — Long-form, short-form, and visual — with brand voice and editorial guardrails.
3. **Distribution & social** — AI-assisted publishing, scheduling, and engagement.
4. **Analytics & attribution** — Multi-touch, post-cookie, AI-influenced.
5. **Personalization & lifecycle** — Email, on-site, in-product.
6. **Audience intelligence** — Listening, sentiment, intent.
7. **Creative production** — Video, image, audio at production scale.

## The Three To Skip

- **AI SDR tools** — the moat evaporated; the conversion data is bad.
- **Standalone AI image generators** — folded into the design platforms.
- **AI SEO "content spinners"** — actively punished by both Google and the AI engines.

## The Only Investments Worth Making This Quarter

- AISO infrastructure (entity graph, structured data, citation monitoring)
- One editorial-grade content platform with brand voice controls
- One analytics platform that handles AI-influenced attribution

That's it. Everything else is a 2026 conversation.
